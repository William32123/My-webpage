How does a team win a Championship?

Every season, the 16 teams with the best records face off for a chance to win the NBA Championship. 8 teams from the east face off against each other and likewise for 8 teams in the west. It is usually a series out of 7 games. Whichever team wins 4 games first, advances to the next stage. In the end, the top team in the east and the top team in the west face off in tthe finals. The team that wins is considered the NBA Champions. They get the Larry O'brien trophy (Championship trophy). The top performer throughout all the games is named as the Finals MVP and he gets his own MVP trophy. The team also gets custom made rings which they recieve a few months later.

Teams with the most Championships

Basktball has been around for a long time. However, there are a couple of teams that have won the most championships in NBA history. The two teams that have the most championships won are the Los Angeles Lakers and the Boston Celtics.

Most recent being the Lakers as they won a championship in the year of 2020.

Currently, both teams have 17 championships which is way more than any other team in the NBA. The two closest teams to the Lakers and Celtics are the Chicago Bulls and the Golden State Warriors. Both of those teams are sitting at 6 championships.
