2021 NBA Champions

The NBA finals in 2021 was between the Phoenix Suns and the Milwaukee Bucks. The series lasted for 6 games. Milwaukee won game 6 with a final score of 105-98. The series ended witha record of 4 games won to 2. The finals MVP was none other than Giannis Antetokounmpo. That was Milwaukee's first ever championship. They are the current defending champions of the NBA.
